The Alchemist TheAlchemist.jpg Original Brazilian publication Author Paulo Coelho Original title O Alquimista Cover artist Caravaggio, "Narcissus", 1597-9 Country Brazil Language Portuguese Genre Quest, adventure, fantasy Publisher HarperTorch (English translation) Publication date 1988 Published in English 1993 Media type Print (hardback, paperback and iTunes) Pages 163 pp (first English edition, hardcover), 208 pages (25th Anniversary Edition) ISBN 0-06-250217-4 (first English edition, hardcover) OCLC 26857452 Dewey Decimal 869.3 Preceded by The Pilgrimage (1987) Followed by Brida (1990) The Alchemist (Portuguese: O Alquimista) is a novel by Brazilian author Paulo Coelho that was first published in 1988.
